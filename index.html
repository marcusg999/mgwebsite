About Me

"Web Design is an art form soaked in the raw prowess to influence and inspire action. It's the face and facade of every brand reaching to connect with new hearts." - Marcus Gray

Marcus Gray began as an art and theology student. Reaching for novel ways to give texture to his intuitive understanding of the nature of reality. Design quickly became a large avenue for presenting his evolving world view. After Working for 15 years in the Film & television Industry as an Art director, writer and entreprenuer. Marcus developed the insight, confidence and resourcefulness needed to bring any vision to life. His meticulous inquisitive nature melds well with any challenge. He now is using his skill as a programmer/web designer to continue his work as a creative entreprenuer and team member in the tech community. Contributing a cutting edge perspective, urban design esthethetic and the technical skills to make products from a refreshing point of view.
