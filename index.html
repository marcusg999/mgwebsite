About Me

"The aim of art is to represent not the outward appearance of things, but their inward significance." ― Aristotle

Marcus Gray began as an art and theology student. Reaching for novel ways to give texture to his intuitive understanding of the nature of reality. Design and writing quickly became a large avenue for presenting his evolving world view. After Working for 15 years in the Film & television Industry as an Art director, writer and entreprenuer Marcus has developed the insight, confidence and resourcefulness needed to bring any vision to life. His meticulous, creative, inquisitive nature melds well with any challenge. He now is using his skill as a programmer/web designer to continue his work as a creative entreprenuer and team member in the tech community. Contributing a cutting edge perspective, urban design aesthetic and the technical skills to create products that solve problems from a refreshing point of view .
