About Me

"Web Design is an art form soaked in the raw prowess to infulence and inspire action. Its the face and facade of every brand reaching to connect with new clients." - Marcus Gray

Marcus Gray began as an art and theology student. Reaching for novel ways to give texture to his intuitive understanding of the nature of reality. Design quickly became a large avenue for presenting his evolving world view. After Working 15 years in the Film & television Industry as an Art director, writer and entreprenuer, Marcus developed the insight and knowledge needed to bring any vision to life. He saw web design as a powerful medium to continue his work as a creative entreprenuer.
